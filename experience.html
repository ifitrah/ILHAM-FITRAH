On 2018, when I was 18 years old back then, I just bought my own phone which is iPhone 6 Plus. I worked for my aunt
and save money to buy the phone. I am shocked because that is my first time having a phone. So, I started to explore
the phone all by myself and get to know one gaming app which is PUBG Mobile. At first, I felt like I would not manage to
play the game as it is a shooting game that would consider to have skills to play. But, as I played everyday and
practicing day by day. In 4 years, which is now in 2022. I managed to play like easily, but not that I can play like
a professional gamer. PUBG Mobile is like a stress ball to me, as I play it when I got stress with my assignments and studies.
People always asked me "How can you not get bored by just playing that game?", I said to them "Each person have their own
interest. Some of them love to practice makeup or even styling hair just to overcome their stress, and so do I!
I love playing games!".

I have invested too much money just by playing games, as I usually bought some of the 'cool' outfits or skins
in PUBG Mobile. It is like an addictive habits for gamers to buy lots of stuff from there. So, it is very difficult
for us to leave the game easily. Besides, through mobile games, I get to know sorts of people from many countries
such as India, China, Bangladesh and many more. They are all friendly, but at the same time they can be very toxic to others.
I have experienced many things by playing PUBG Mobile, some are sweet and some are bitter. I guess that is life all about then.
